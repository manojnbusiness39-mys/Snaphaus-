Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How much does interior design cost in Mysore?

It depends on the size of the space, the materials you choose and how much work is involved. After a free consultation and site visit, we give you a clear, itemised quote with no hidden charges.

Do you handle turnkey interiors — both design and execution?

Yes. We take a project from the first idea to the final handover — design, 3D visualisation, carpentry, false ceiling, painting, electrical and finishing — all managed by one team.

Which areas in and around Mysore do you serve?

We work across Mysuru — Vijayanagar, Kuvempunagar, Gokulam, Jayalakshmipuram, Saraswathipuram, Hebbal, Bogadi, JP Nagar and more — plus nearby towns on request.

How long does a full-home interior take?

A typical 2–3 BHK turnkey interior takes about 45 to 75 days depending on scope. You get a committed timeline before work begins.

Do you design office and commercial spaces too?

Yes — we design offices, clinics, retail stores and other commercial spaces alongside homes and apartments.

Can I see a 3D design before the work starts?

Absolutely. We share realistic 3D visuals so you can approve the look, materials and colours before any execution begins.
